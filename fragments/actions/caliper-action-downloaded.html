Downloaded

The Downloaded action signals that a copy of the resource has been transferred from a central or primary system to a remote or secondary system. Inverse of Uploaded.

Downloaded
WordNet® Gloss
Transfer a file or program from a central computer to a smaller computer or to a computer at a remote location. Inverse of Uploaded.
